IMPORTANCE OF HALLMARKING CENTRE

The BIS Hallmark certifies the purity of gold jewellery, playing a crucial role in building consumer trust. The introduction of mandatory hallmarking for gold jewellery in India marks a significant move toward greater transparency and consumer protection. Currently, there are around 1,500 hallmarking centres operating across the country, with an estimated 1,500 to 2,000 more expected to be established in the near future.
